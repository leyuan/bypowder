General Information

Polyester series powder is made by carboxyl polyester resin. It is designed for exterior products. For example, outdoor shell for electronics, building decorating, road guardrail, outdoor furniture, outdoor fencings, and outdoor air conditioning unit etc. According to customer’s need, we provide two options (TGIC and TGIC-free) for our customers.

Products Series

Polyester powder coatings have a huge selections in colors and glosses. We have high gloss (above 80%), semi-gloss (50 – 80%), matt black (20 – 50%) and flat black (below 20%) Polyester powder coatings. Gloss rate can be customized accordingly.

Physical Properties

Specific gravity: 1.4 – 1.6 g/cm³
Grain fineness distribution: 100% smaller than 100um (can be adjusted accordingly)

Curing Condition

Standard: 190°C (work piece temperature), 15min

Average Coverage

8-11 m²/kg @ film thickness 70um (2.76mil)
Note: Number is calculated under 100% usage rate.

General Information

Epoxy series powder is made by epoxy resin and other chemical resistance material, the product has excellent chemical resistance and mechanical properties. Our Epoxy powder coating can be applied to metal products that require high chemical resistance, excellent electrical isolation and outstanding flexibility. Transportation pipes, chemical equipment, or indoor furniture are the top choices for using our Epoxy powder coatings. We do not recommend of using our Epoxy powders on outdoor products directly.

Products Series

We provide standard curing and low temperature curing, both type of Epoxy powder coatings.

Physical Properties

Specific gravity: 1.4 – 1.6 g/cm³
Grain fineness distribution: 100% smaller than 100um (can be adjusted accordingly)

Curing Condition

A Type: 200°C (work piece temperature), 10min
B Type: 180°C (work piece temperature), 15min
C Type: 140°C (work piece temperature), 20min

Average Coverage

9-12 m²/kg @ film thickness 60um (2.36mil)
Note: Number is calculated under 100% usage rate.

Epoxy-Polyester Hybrids powder coatings can be made in a wide varieties of colours and glosses, it can be applied to any type of indoor metal products.

General Information

Antimicrobial powder coatings are made of inorganic antimicrobial functional material which is harmless to human body. This series of powder coatings have excellent antimicrobial, reliability, safety, and long residual action. Our Antimicrobial Powder Coatings have the same coating properties and spraying process as the normal powder coatings. According to total number of bacteria test results, our Antimicrobial Powder Coatings inhibit a broad types of strain effectively. For example, Sarcina Lutea, Bacillus Subtilis, Staphylococcus Aureus, Colon Bacillus, Aspergillus Flavus, and Penicillium Citrinum etc.

Products Series

Antimicrobial powder coatings should be applied to household appliances, steel made furniture, kitchen supplies, medical apparatus, office supplies and outdoor recreation facilities etc.
