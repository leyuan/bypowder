AAMA STANDARDS

The American Architectural Manufacturer Association known as AAMA is the leading trade association that representing the high standard of quality certification for manufacturers and suppliers.

We developed our high quality powder coatings to comply with AAMA 2603 specifications. According to customer’s requests, we are able to further enhance the durability of our powder coatings, therefore to comply with AAMA 2604 and 2605 specifications.

Our Super Durable Powder Coatings are formulated with high performance powder coating resins and top grade pigments. We carefully choose the main ingredients that make up the recipe for our powder coatings. Our Super Durable Powder Coatings perform extremely well aesthetically and in durability which makes it widely used in architectural applications.

We also have a huge selection of colors and textures for our customer to choose. Each product series comes in a wide range of powder coat colors and in different gloss rate.
