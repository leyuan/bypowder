General Information

Beyond Powder Coatings Ltd. offers superior performance powder coatings for many industrial applications. Our high quality powder coatings are presented in a wide range of different technologies such as: Polyester TGIC, Polyester TGIC-Free, Epoxies, Polyester-Epoxy Hybrids, Polyurethane, Art Style, High Temperature Resistance, and Antimicrobial Coatings. We also provide many types of surface appearance finishes, including Smooth, Texture, Sand Texture, River Texture, Silk Texture, Marbling Texture, Metallic, and Hammer Tone etc. We take a lot of pride in our product not only because of the superior quality but also the price and service that we offer. We treat customer needs as our first priority, your feedback is valuable and important to us. Beyond Powder Coatings truly appreciate the opportunity to serve our customers.
