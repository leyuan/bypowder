General Information

Polyester series powder is made by carboxyl polyester resin. It is designed for exterior products. For example, outdoor shell for electronics, building decorating, road guardrail, outdoor furniture, outdoor fencings, and outdoor air conditioning unit etc. According to customer’s need, we provide two options (TGIC and TGIC-free) for our customers.

Products Series

Polyester powder coatings have a huge selections in colors and glosses. We have high gloss (above 80%), semi-gloss (50 – 80%), matt black (20 – 50%) and flat black (below 20%) Polyester powder coatings. Gloss rate can be customized accordingly.

Physical Properties

Specific gravity: 1.4 – 1.7 g/cm3
Grain fineness distribution: 100% smaller than 100um (can be adjusted accordingly)

Curing Condition

Standard: 190oC (work piece temperature), 15min

Physical Properties

8-11 m2/kg @ film thickness 70um (2.76mil)
Note: Number is calculated under 100% usage rate.
